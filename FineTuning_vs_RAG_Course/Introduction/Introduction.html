Fine-Tuning vs. RAG

Course Overview

One of the most frequent questions in AI architecture is: "Should I fine-tune my model or use RAG?" This course provides a comprehensive comparison of these two distinct approaches (along with Prompt Engineering) to help you make informed architectural decisions. We will explore the mechanics, costs, trade-offs, and a practical decision matrix for real-world applications.

Learning Objectives

  • Differentiate between Prompt Engineering, RAG, and Fine-Tuning.
  • Understand the technical workflow of a RAG pipeline.
  • Identify the specific scenarios where Fine-Tuning is superior to RAG.
  • Apply the "Decision Matrix" to choose the right strategy for a project.
  • Evaluate the cost and complexity of each approach.

Expected Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to architect robust AI systems by selecting the most efficient method for providing a model with specialized knowledge and behavior.

Target Audience

  • AI Solution Architects
  • Machine Learning Engineers
  • Technical Product Managers
  • Full-stack Developers building AI features

Prerequisites

  • Intermediate understanding of LLMs.
  • Basic knowledge of vector databases and embedding concepts.
  • Familiarity with the concept of model training/weights.

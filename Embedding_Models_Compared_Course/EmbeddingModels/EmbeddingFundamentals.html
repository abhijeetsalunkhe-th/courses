Embedding Fundamentals and Benchmarks

What Are Text Embeddings?

Text embeddings are dense vector representations of text that capture semantic meaning. Similar texts produce similar vectors, enabling semantic search, clustering, and classification.

MTEB Benchmark Results

The Massive Text Embedding Benchmark (MTEB) evaluates models across retrieval, classification, clustering, and semantic similarity tasks:

  • Retrieval (NDCG@10): Voyage-3 and OpenAI large consistently lead
  • Classification: Cohere embed-v3 excels in cross-lingual tasks
  • Clustering: BGE models perform well for topic clustering
  • Semantic Similarity: Most modern models are competitive

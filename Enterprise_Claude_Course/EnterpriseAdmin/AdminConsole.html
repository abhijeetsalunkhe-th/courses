Hierarchy: Organization vs. Workspaces

Enterprise Claude uses a hierarchical structure to manage data and users:

  • Organization: The top-level entity representing the entire company. Controls billing, SSO, and global settings.
  • Workspaces: Isolated environments within the organization (e.g., "Marketing", "Research", "Legal"). Data in one workspace is not shared with others by default.

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

RoleCapabilities
Org AdminFull control over the organization, billing, and all workspaces.

Billing AdminView and manage invoices and payment methods only.

Managing Provisioning

Admins can choose how users are added to the organization:

  • Manual Invite: Direct email invitation (good for small teams).
  • Domain Capture: Automatically add any user with a company email address (@yourcompany.com).
  • SCIM Provisioning: Automated sync with Okta or Azure AD (recommended for 50+ users).

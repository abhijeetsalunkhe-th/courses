Gemini for Google Workspace

Welcome to Gemini for Google Workspace - Docs, Sheets, and Gmail. This course explores how to integrate Google's most powerful AI directly into your daily productivity workflow. You will learn how to automate mundane tasks, generate high-quality content, and derive deep insights from your data using natural language.

Learning Objectives

  • Master AI-assisted writing in Google Docs and Gmail to create drafts and refine content.
  • Leverage Gemini in Sheets for automated data organization, formula generation, and analysis.
  • Create multi-modal presentations in Google Slides using AI-generated imagery and outlines.
  • Understand Enterprise controls for data privacy, compliance, and administrative management.
  • Apply advanced prompting to create custom Google Apps Script automations.

Target Audience

This course is designed for:

  • Business Professionals looking to increase their productivity and output quality.
  • Data Analysts seeking faster ways to organize and interpret spreadsheet data.
  • Workspace Administrators responsible for deploying and managing AI features in their organization.

Prerequisites

Participants should have a basic familiarity with Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Gmail). No prior AI or programming experience is required.

Course Structure

  • Module 1: AI-Powered Productivity: Deep dive into Docs, Slides, and Gmail integrations.
  • Module 2: Sheets and Enterprise Automation: Advanced spreadsheet techniques and admin considerations.
  • Pros and Cons: Evaluating the benefits and limitations of AI in the workplace.
  • Final Assessment: A comprehensive evaluation of your Gemini expertise.

You need to score 70% or higher on each assessment to proceed.
